A/B Compare Controls

Use this feature to easily compare two different tweaks of your pedalboard (e.g., testing a different distortion level or delay time) without losing your original settings.

  • Camera Icon (Take Snapshot): Captures your current pedalboard settings and saves them into both slot A and slot B to give you a common starting point.
  • Button A (Switch to A): Loads the snapshot stored in A, while temporarily holding your current active setup in slot B so you don't lose your adjustments.
  • Button B (Switch to B): Loads the snapshot stored in B, while temporarily holding your current active setup in slot A so you don't lose your adjustments.
